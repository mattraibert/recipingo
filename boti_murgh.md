Boti Murgh
==========

Chicken in 8 pcs 750 g
Yogurt 30 g
Red chile powder 3 g
Salt 3 g
Chaat masala 3 g
Ginger paste 6 g
Garlic paste 18 g
Juice of a lemon 1 ea
Melted butter 50 g

Mix all except butter and marinate for 2 hr
Brush with butter and grill or bake at 350F for 30 mins
Serve with chutney and naan
