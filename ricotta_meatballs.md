Ricotta Meatballs
=================

1 lb ground chuck
1 lb ground pork
.5 lb ricotta
3 eggs
2 cups parmesean cheese
Taragon
lemon zest
.5 lb breadcrumbs

Instructions:
-------------

Mix all
fry in oo 3 at a time for around 2 minutes constantly turning
poach in basic tomato sauce for 10 more mins
